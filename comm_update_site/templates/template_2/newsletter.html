UPCOMING EVENTS - EHOUSE AT REVOLVE CONFERENCE
 
eHouse's founder and creative director, Aaron Quinn, will be speaking at the upcoming Revolve Conference on October 29 & 30. Revolve self-describes as the "Lowcountry's gathering of designers, marketers and entrepreneurs who are influencing what’s next, right now." Aaron will be giving a talk on the importance of prototyping as a necessary part of a successful project strategy and one that you can't afford to skip. He'll be speaking at 3:30 pm on October 29. We'd love to see you there! For more information on the conference and the rest of the speakers, visit revolveconference.com.
