Welcome to C Madrid!

The Tower of Glass (‘Torre de Cristal’ in Spanish) is a skyscraper in the Cuatro Torres Business Area in Madrid, completed in 2008 with a final height of 249 meters. Cerner has opened its office in 2016 on the 12th floor. The location is few minutes’ walk from underground and taxi stands, surrounded by restaurants and coffee shops, with a hotel just outside.
Achievements

CONGRATULATIONS TEAM SPAIN!

Fundación Alares is a National Award for the Reconciliation of Work, Family, Personal Life, and Social Responsibility given by Spanish non-profit organization dedicated to the development of social improvement projects. This award, chaired by the royal family, recognized the completeness and comprehensiveness of the work-life balance policies implemented by the Cerner team in Spain over the past years and will certainly help form our practices going forward. This was a highly-contested award in which Cerner was selected from over 350 applications.
